# Privacy Policy for FileSeekAI

**Last updated: June 28, 2026**

FileSeekAI ("the App") is a local file search utility for macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Data Collection and Transmission
FileSeekAI does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or user information.
- All search indexing, semantic analysis, and file query operations are performed **entirely on your local Mac**.
- The App does not connect to any external server or cloud service.
- No files, folder structures, or search terms ever leave your device.

## 2. Permissions Used
To function properly, FileSeekAI requires access to specific folders on your system:
- **Folder Access (Desktop, Documents, Downloads, or custom directories)**: Only used to index files so that you can search them. This access is local-only and subject to macOS System Sandbox permissions.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking services.

## 4. Updates to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted directly within this repository.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, please contact us.
